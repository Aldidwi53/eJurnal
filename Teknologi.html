Pengembangan IT

JPIT (Jurnal Informatika: Jurnal Pengembangan IT), with registered number ISSN 2477-5126 (print), 2548-9356 (online) is a scientific journal managed by Program Studi D4 Teknik Informatika Politeknik Harapan Bersama and published by Pusat Penelitian dan Pengabdian Masyarakat (P3M) Politeknik Harapan Bersama Tegal. JPIT is a peer-reviewed journal published three times a year (January, May and September).
